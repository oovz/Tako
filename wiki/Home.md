## What is Tako?

Tako turns Chrome's Side Panel into a manga command center. Browse a series, pick the chapters you want, queue them, and export ready-to-read files in one workflow. No extra tabs. No save-dialog spam. No one-by-one image downloads.

## Why Tako wins

- **Browser-native workflow** — everything happens inside Chrome's Side Panel, right next to your reading tab.
- **Batch queue with retry** — queue dozens of chapters and let Tako handle retries automatically.
- **Clean exports** — CBZ, ZIP, or image folders with custom path and filename templates.
- **Curated site support** — optimized integrations for MangaDex, Pixiv Comic, Shonen Jump+, Manhuagui, and Comic Nettai.
- **Privacy-first** — no analytics, no telemetry, no data collection.
- **Open source** — MIT license, full source on GitHub.
